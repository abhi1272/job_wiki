```text
4. CRITICAL GAP IDENTIFICATION (Spend 2 minutes finding weak spots):

     "While my experience is focused on [My Strengths], I have [Conceptual/Related Experience] in [Gap Area] and can adapt quickly by..."

   - Summarise changes needed in the Professional Profile section: 
============================================================
```

## 💡 Real-World Example: Tailoring for a "Senior Staff Engineer (Fintech Startup)"

Here is how you would fill out the worksheet for a Fintech team looking for a React/Node backend expert to scale transaction ledger pipelines:

* **Key Requirements:** `NestJS`, `PostgreSQL`, `TypeScript`, `Kafka`.
* **Seniority Scan:** Mentoring junior developers, leading system design reviews.
* **My Advantage Project:** **ITBoost Stabilization** (ConnectWise) and **No-Code Agent Builder** (Concentrix). Why? The ledger application needs database deadlock resolution and event streaming (Kafka)—exactly what you built.
* **Critical Gap:** The company uses AWS, but your recent GKE experience is GCP-heavy.
* **Mitigation Hook:** 
  > "While my recent platform deployments have been focused on Google Cloud Platform and GKE, the underlying Kubernetes services, Helm chart package structures, and containerization patterns are identical, and I have previous hands-on experience managing EC2, S3, and WAF rules on AWS."
* **Resume Tweak:** Update the top line of your summary to read: *"11 years of experience building high-throughput microservices using TypeScript, NestJS, and Kafka event streams..."*
