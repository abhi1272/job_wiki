# Interview Day Checklists

Use these checklists to ensure you are fully prepared, both logistically and mentally, before your interview.

[← Back to Index](../README.md) | [← Previous: Reverse Interview Questions](./Questions_To_Ask.md)

---

## 🗓️ 1. The Day-Before Checklist

Complete these tasks the evening before your interview:

- [ ] **Resume Review:** Read through the specific resume version you submitted for this role. Ensure you remember the exact metric points (e.g. *35% latency drop, 40% cost reduction*).
- [ ] **Tech Setup Check:**
  - Verify your webcam and microphone are working.
  - Test the video platform link (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, Webex).
  - Ensure your internet speed is stable. Set up a mobile hotspot backup.
- [ ] **Work Environment Prep:**
  - Clean up your background area.
  - Set up lighting so your face is clearly visible.
  - Inform family/flatmates of the interview times to avoid interruptions.
- [ ] **Readiness Review:** Review the [Interview Cheatsheet](./Cheatsheet.md) and practice the **60-Second Introduction** twice out loud.
- [ ] **Sleep:** Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep. Avoid late-night cramming.

---

## ☀️ 2. The Morning-Of Checklist

Complete these tasks in the hours leading up to the interview:

- [ ] **System Reboot:** Restart your laptop to clear memory allocations, close background software, and pause system updates.
- [ ] **Logistics Check:**
  - Open the interview link 5 minutes early.
  - Keep a glass of water nearby.
  - Keep a pen and a notepad on your desk for system design sketches or notes.
- [ ] **Workspace Setup:**
  - Close all browser tabs except the coding workspace (if applicable) and the interview window.
  - Put your phone on silent mode.
- [ ] **Quick Review:** Spend 5 minutes reviewing the core [One-Liners](./Cheatsheet.md) for the AI Gateway and Agent Builder.
- [ ] **Mental Prep:** Take a few deep breaths. Remind yourself that you have 11 years of solid systems engineering experience, and you built these platforms successfully. You are ready.
